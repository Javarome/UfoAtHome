Ufology is a research domain that aims to study UFO phenomena. It includes various tendencies, ranging from rationalists to true believers in an extraterrestrial explanation, including also more moderate people, and even scientists. The tools from this site are designed to help all these people to work more efficiently, promoting data sharing, redudancy reduction and an improved efficiency in general.
