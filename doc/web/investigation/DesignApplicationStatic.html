Basically UFO@home can be viewed as the stacking of three layers :

  1. UFO@home, which is the application accessible to the public. It manages user interactions issues, user contexts, specific features such as statistics computing, etc.
  2. the RR0 model, which modelize the UFO domain, at high (cases, accounts, classifications) and low-level (events, moments).
  3. the Prosper engine (maybe), which is not part of the UFO@home project, but a standalone project intended to provide artificial intelligence capabilities to any application, allowing to predict facts depending of ancien facts, identify events patterns, etc. As UFO@home is a good candidate to experiment Prosper, Prosper has been identified as a pluggable and valuable component of the UFO@home.
